D.4.1 StructureDefinition: DAF-AllergyIntolerance

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/daf-allergyintolerance

Defines constraints and extensions on the AllergyIntolerance resource for use in querying and retrieving allergy information.

This profile was published on Thu, Aug 21, 2014 00:00+0000 as a draft by Health Level Seven International (Infrastructure and Messaging - Data Access Framework).
