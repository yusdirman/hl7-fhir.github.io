H.?? StructureDefinition: HSPC-Organization

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/hspc-organization

The "Organization" model describes an organization. A Provider Organization is a role played by an instance of this organization.

This profile was published on Tue, May 19, 2015 00:00+0000 as a draft by HSPC.
