A.0 US Lab Implementation Guides defined as part of FHIR

The US Laboratory Order Implementation Guides consists of three specifications: 1) laboratory orders from the ambulatory care setting to the laboratory, 2) laboratory results from the laboratory to provider in ambulatory care setting and 3) laboratory results from the laboratory to the local public health jurisdiction. These implementation guides have been prepared as a U.S. Realm Specification on behalf of the HL7 Orders and Observation Workgroup. For full details please refer to USLAB FHIR IG Table of Contents.
