A.12.2 StructureDefinition: USlab-ReasonForStudy

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/uslab-reasonforstudy

The Condition(s) referenced in USRealm laboratory test and reporting in ambulatory care setting.

This profile was published on Mon, Nov 17, 2014 00:00+0000 as a draft by Health Level Seven International (Orders and Observations - US Lab).
